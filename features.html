Technical Specifications

  • Weight: 6 kg
  • Power Requirements for Movement: 12V, 3.75A
  • Power Requirements for Arm: 12V, 7.5A
  • Microcontroller: Arduino UNO R3
  • App Controller for Movement: Bluetooth
  • App Controller for Arm: My Luna Picker application
  • Programming Language: C and C++

User Guide

Follow these steps to get started with Luna Picker:

  1. Power on the robot.
  2. Connect to the control interface via your smartphone.
  3. Open My Luna Picker application on your phone.
  4. Configure the task settings.
  5. Start the operation and monitor progress.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Luna Picker?

Luna Picker is an Arduino-powered robotic assistant designed for picking and moving objects with precision, using a robotic arm.

Who can use Luna Picker?

Luna Picker is ideal for elderly individuals, people with disabilities, and those needing assistance in daily tasks.

What features does Luna Picker offer?

Luna Picker features self-navigation, a functional robotic arm, and the ability to pick, hold, and place items safely.

Is Luna Picker customizable?

Yes, Luna Picker can be customized to adapt to specific needs, such as different arm attachments or task-specific programming.

What types of objects can Luna Picker handle?

It can handle lightweight objects like square sponge , and small tools, depending on the arm attachment.

Does Luna Picker require technical knowledge to operate?

No, it is user-friendly and can be operated via a simple remote control by using my Luna Picker application.

What is the power source for Luna Picker?

Luna Picker is powered by a rechargeable battery and can operate for several hours on a single charge.
